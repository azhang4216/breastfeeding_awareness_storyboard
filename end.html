A Note From Developers & More Resources

We hope that after experiencing early motherhood in the shoes of 4 different mothers, you have come to realize the world of differences some have to face. We urge you to think about the new moms in your life, and how their situations may impact their ability to breastfeed and care for their baby.

We hope you now have a better understanding of the choices that different moms can and cannot make. Having learned more about the difficulties that mothers have to face in order to breastfeed, consider donating to related non-profits or learning more via the Learn More button below.

US Breastfeeding Committee: A coalition of more than 100 organizations that support the mission to drive collaborative efforts for policy and practices that create a landscape of breastfeeding support across the United States.

La Leche League International: An international effort to help mothers breastfeed through mother-to-mother support, encouragement, information, and education, and to promote a better understanding of breastfeeding as an important element in the healthy development of the baby and mother.

Black Mothers’ Breastfeeding Association: A national non-profit organization that is focused on fostering a national impact on the reduction of racial disparities in breastfeeding success for black families.

HelpMe Feed: A mobile app developed for health professionals and mothers to improve global breastfeeding rates.

Fed Is Best: A foundation that works to identify critical gaps in current breastfeeding protocols, guidelines, and education programs. They do so by providing families and health professionals with the most up-to-date scientific research, education, and resources to practice safe infant feeding with breast milk, formula, or a combination of both.
